# Impeccable Design Context — Reap Capital

## Design Context

### Users
Prospective and current limited partners — sophisticated (and select non-accredited) investors evaluating a Dallas–Fort Worth value-add multifamily operator. They arrive skeptical, comparing sponsors; they read on desktop at work and on phones in the evening. The job to be done: "convince me this operator is disciplined, proven, and worth trusting with capital" → request access / contact.

### Brand Personality
Institutional but plainspoken. Confident, declarative, first-person plural ("We purchase underperforming assets…"). Proof over adjectives — numbers do the persuading (34.25% IRR, $226M AUM); every stat specific, never rounded to vagueness. Never: emoji, exclamation points, hype words.

### Aesthetic Direction
Editorial/luxury-restrained. Two olives (#394334 primary, #4F5B49), one gold (#CEB47F/#CCAF79 — accent metal, never large fills), off-white #F3F3F3, white, black. Playfair Display SC (regular, never bold) display over Montserrat body. Square edges (0 radius; 2px max on controls). Olive scrims over aerial/architectural photography; 10%-white eagle watermark as the only texture. Flat by default; hairline borders; shadows only to lift interactive cards. Restrained motion (no bounces, no parallax). Icons: none beyond typed arrows (⟶ →) and the eagle glyph.

### Design Principles
1. Numbers persuade; adjectives don't. Lead with specific, verifiable metrics.
2. Gold is rare. Its power comes from scarcity against olive and off-white.
3. Serif/sans contrast carries hierarchy — headings are never bold.
4. Square, flat, left-aligned, asymmetric. Whitespace is the luxury signal.
5. Photography always sits under olive; white/gold type must stay legible.
