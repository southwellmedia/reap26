## The thesis

Liberty Park was not a heavy renovation story. It was 67 units in Dallas,
bought direct from the seller — no brokered process, no auction dynamics
bidding the basis up. The deal was underwritten as a stabilized high-yield
play: buy well, operate well, and let the income do the work.

## The acquisition

Direct-from-seller deals are won on relationships and certainty of close, not
on the highest number in a bid sheet. Sourcing off-market is one of the
quieter advantages of operating in one market for years — the call comes to
you.

## Execution

With no repositioning program to run, the plan was operational discipline:
professional management, tight expenses, and steady income on a basis that
made the yield attractive from day one.

## The outcome

Full cycle in 23 months — a 2.05x equity multiple and a 49% actual IRR to our
partners. Proof that the return profile comes from the basis and the
execution, not from how dramatic the business plan sounds.
