Everyone wants the secret. The honest answer is that there isn't one — there is a process, executed the same way every time, by people who have done it before.

We evaluate markets top-down, we underwrite conservatively, and we keep our lender and broker relationships warm long before we need them. When a genuine opportunity surfaces — a forced seller, an expired rate cap, a mismanaged asset — the buyer who wins is the one who is already prepared to close.

That preparation is the whole game. The rest is patience.
