Every offering memorandum in this industry says "value-add." Very few sponsors can tell you their per-unit turn cost from last month, or how many days a unit sits between move-out and rent-ready.

We can, because the construction team executing the scope sits inside the same company as the team that underwrote it. A standardized renovation scope, repeated across hundreds of units, is what turns a projection into a rent roll.

The discipline is boring. The results are not.
